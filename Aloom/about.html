About us

How we got started

I’ve experienced firsthand what happens when a family member passes away or becomes unable to manage their day to day affairs and important information such as how bills are managed, the location of financial assets, family health history or details of final wishes is not available. From this experience I realized I didn’t have a good understanding of my own family’s important life information. If something were to happen to my husband, I don’t know how the bills get paid, where all of our bank accounts and investments are located, information about our insurance policies and the list goes on. What scared me even more was thinking about the nightmare our kids would face if something were to happen to both of us. Even worse, where would WE find critical information if something were to happen to one of our kids!


I started making a list of the important information we had been leaving to ‘memory’ and soon realized, it is a BIG list. I talked to other people about how they manage their important information and realized they had the same problems. I came to realize that if I needed to figure out what information was required and create an easy way to record and share it with family members, so did a lot of other people.


Using my personal life experiences and experience from my 30 year career in IT, I engaged a software development company to create the “Say for A Moment™” software. It is a low cost, easy to use solution that steps you through the data gathering process and enables you to easily share your information with other family members.


In addition to helping others avoid making the same costly mistakes we had experienced because we did not have all the necessary information, we are also committed to helping our community by donating 10% of our gross sales to various charities.”

Our Mission

To educate and empower families with the knowledge and tools to capture their critical data so they are prepared for life’s events.

Our Vision

Peace of Mind.

Our Core Values

Give back to the community through our commitment to raise funds for charities that help to assist and protect others who are less fortunate.
