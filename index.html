    The Problem

    The amount of personal information has increased substantially over the past few decades but most of us still manage our information the same way we always have . . . we leave it to memory.


    Remembering is hard enough, but the real challenge is how to make this information available to other family members.If you have ever managed another person’s daily affairs or been an executor, you know it’s a nightmare when you are missing important information. We have experienced this problem first hand and as a result we created software to:

    • Identify what information needs to be saved and shared
    • Record information and attach related files and documents
    • Share day to day information without sharing sensitive information.

    Our mission is to provide peace of mind by helping to ensure life’s important information is available whenever it is needed. We are also committed to helping our community by donating 10% of our gross sales to charity.
